Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Welcome to IGDownloader ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit IGDownloader.app or use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum amount of data required to operate the service:

  • URLs you submit: The Instagram post or reel links you paste into the downloader. These are used only to process your download request and are not stored after processing.
  • Usage data: Standard server logs may include your IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This data is used for security monitoring and service improvement only.
  • Cookies: We may use minimal analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand traffic patterns. No personal profile is built from this data.

2. What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your Instagram username or password.
  • We do not store copies of the videos you download.
  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

3. How We Use Your Information

Any data collected is used strictly to:

  • Process your video download requests.
  • Maintain and improve the security and performance of the service.
  • Analyze aggregate (anonymized) traffic for product improvements.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service may use the following third-party tools:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymized, aggregate website traffic analysis.
  • Cloudflare: DDoS protection and CDN routing.
  • yt-dlp: An open-source utility used server-side to fetch video data from public Instagram URLs.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Retention

We do not persistently store the URLs you submit or the content you download. Server access logs are typically purged within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

IGDownloader is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access, deletion, or correction of any personal data we hold. Since we collect minimal data, most requests can be fulfilled automatically by simply not using the service. For any concerns, contact us at our contact page.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Us page.
